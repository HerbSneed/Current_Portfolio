ABOUT
ME

My name is Herb Sneed. I'm a veteran of the United States Air Force, where I served in North Carolina, Saudi Arabia and South Korea. After my enlistment, I enrolled into East Carolina University were I majored in Mass Communication with a focus in Media Production. Upon graduation in 2006, I moved to Atlanta, Georgia where I produced content for non-profits and start-up companies. After four years in Atlanta I moved to San Francisco and attended the Academy of Art Univesity and earned a MFA in Film; focusing in Film Editing and Visual Effects. Once I graduated, I relocated to Los Angeles and worked for nearly 10 years in the entertainment industry on shows like, Big Brother, Ridiculousness, A Million Little Things and RuPaul Drag Race; just to name a few. Looking for something new and exciting I decided to take a stab at software development and enrolled into the UNC Chapel Hill Coding Bootcamp.
